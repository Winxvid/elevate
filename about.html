OUR STORY

Founded in November 2024 by car and art enthusiast Miranda Reyes, Elevated Auto Wraps represents a significant milestone in the Texas automotive landscape as the state's first woman-owned and operated vehicle wrap studio.

Miranda launched Elevated with a singular vision: to redefine automotive customization through a lens of artistic precision and technical excellence. Her background as a lifelong car enthusiast, combined with a meticulous eye for detail, has quickly established the studio as a premier destination for San Antonio’s most discerning owners.

Recently featured in VOYAGE SAN ANTONIO MAGAZINE, Miranda’s journey is rooted in breaking barriers within the industry. From complex color-change PPF on high-performance vehicles to bespoke livery designs, her hands-on approach ensures that every vehicle leaving our studio reflects an uncompromising standard of craft.
