BENEFITS OF
PPF.

Everything you need to know about protecting your investment. Our film provides an invisible shield against the elements.

PPF acts as a durable barrier against chips, scratches, and surface damage caused by everyday driving, preserving your vehicle's factory paint.
Our film self-heals from light scratches and swirl marks with heat from the sun or engine, constantly keeping your finish looking flawless.
Advanced hydrophobic top coats repel water and dirt, making your vehicle significantly easier to wash and keeping it cleaner for longer.
Protects against bird droppings, tree sap, bug splatter, road tar, and harsh chemicals that can otherwise etch or stain your clear coat.
Prevents oxidative fading and sun damage while maintaining absolute transparency so your paint color shines through perfectly.
By keeping your paint in showroom condition, PPF helps maintain the highest possible resale value for your vehicle.
